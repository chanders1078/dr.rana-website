FAQ's

+ When should I see a Cardiologist?

You should see a cardiologist immediately when you face one or more of the below-mentioned symptoms.

  • Pressure/Tightness/Pain in Chest
  • Discomfort in Arms, Back, Neck, Jaw or Stomach
  • Shortness of Breath
  • Extreme Fatigue
  • Cold Sweat
  • Sudden Dizziness/Nausea
